Memories ❤️

Some memories never fade. School functions where you came to support us. The day you first encouraged me to join Bharatanatyam classes. The pride of completing my Salangai Poojai with your support. Festivals filled with laughter, firecrackers, and the confidence you gave us. Learning to ride a bicycle, falling many times, and getting back up because you believed I could do it. Small moments that may seem ordinary to others, but became unforgettable memories for me. Every memory reminds me of how much you have always been there for us.
