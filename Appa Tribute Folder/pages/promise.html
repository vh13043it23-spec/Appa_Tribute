My Promise To You

Appa, All my life, I have seen you work harder than anyone I know. I have seen you leave home during festivals, carry responsibilities without complaining, and put our needs before your own. Today, you still wake up every day and give your best to your duty. But one day, I want that responsibility to become mine. I want to study well. I want to build a successful career. I want to make you proud. Most importantly, I want to give you the peaceful life that you truly deserve. One day, I want you to rest without worries. I want you to spend time doing the things you love. I want to see you smile more and stress less. This is my promise to you. Just like you never gave up on me, I will never give up until I can give you that life.
